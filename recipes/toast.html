Toast

What is toast?

Toast is a delicacy of unknown origin enjoyed worldwide. From the penthouses of the busiest metropolae, to the deepest, darkest, jungles of the Amazon, trillions of people consume this rare delight each and every day.

Toast, colloquially known as Le toast and Ouchy hot fire bread is not a recipe to be attempted by the faint of heart. Here we will walk you through the delicate and intricate process of crafting a heavenly slice of ouchy hot fire bread for your own enjoyment.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Hot
  • Topping (To taste)

Steps

  1. Take a slice of bread.
  2. Put your slice of bread next to hot. Using a toaster is recommended, but any hot will do.
  3. (Optional) If your hot is only on one side, flip your bread over to ensure that both sides are hot and ouchy.
  4. Put topping on now-hot-and-ouchy bread.
  5. Enjoy!
