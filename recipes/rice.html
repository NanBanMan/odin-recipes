Rice

What is Rice?

Rice is white and enjoyed by Japanese people. It is therefore known as "The Tom Cruise of Foods".

Ingredients

  • A phone
  • Water
  • Money (Often sold under the name "Ca$$$h")

Steps

  1. Use the phone to dial the numer of a local Chinese restaurant and order an item off the set menu.
  2. Wait for 20-45 minutes.
  3. Upon receipt of your food delivery, throw everything except the rice into the garbage.
  4. Savor the taste of the Orient!
